About

David Maxwell is a Glasgow based artist and musician. He studied painting at Leith School of Art in Edinburgh in 2013/14 and plays in Glasgow bands’ Gift Horse and The Gymnastic Band.

The work in the current exhibition is from two recent series of paintings: Polaroid Paintings and Indoor Plants. The Polaroid Paintings are based on a series of Polaroid photographs, taken over the last year by the artist's brother of his kitchen in Reykjavik, Iceland. The series Indoor Plants reflects on the personal spaces of the artist's home. Both sets of work are concerned with domestic spaces and the objects we choose to furnish these spaces with.
